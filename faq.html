Common Questions

Are your dice balanced?
Yes. We water‑float test and roll test every set; tolerances are within typical hobby standards.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, to most regions. Shipping costs calculate at checkout.
How should I care for my dice?
Store away from direct sunlight and avoid high heat. Clean gently with a microfiber cloth.
